Frequently Asked Questions

  • What subjects are offered for home tuition?

    We offer a wide range of subjects including Math, Science, English, and more. Our tutors are skilled in various curricula such as CBSE, ICSE, and international systems. If you have a specific subject or curriculum in mind, please let us know!

  • How do I find a suitable tutor for my child?

    Our team carefully matches students with tutors based on their requirements and learning style. You can also review tutor profiles and their qualifications before making a decision. We ensure that all tutors are vetted and experienced.

  • What is the process for scheduling sessions?

    Once you choose a tutor, you can schedule sessions directly through our online booking system. You’ll have the flexibility to choose times that fit your schedule, and you can modify or cancel appointments as needed.

  • Are there any trial sessions available?

    Yes, we offer trial sessions for new students. This allows you to assess the tutor’s compatibility with your child before committing to a full-term plan. Please contact us to schedule a trial session.

  • What are the payment options available?

    We offer various payment options including credit/debit cards, online transfers, and other secure payment methods. You can choose the option that best suits you during the booking process.

  • What happens if a tutor is unavailable for a scheduled session?

    In the unlikely event that a tutor is unavailable, we will notify you as soon as possible and offer alternative arrangements. You can reschedule the session or choose a different tutor if needed.
